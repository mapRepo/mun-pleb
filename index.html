Amalgamation 2016

As part of a public consultation process, on May 28th, 2016 Pictou Country held a plebiscite during which residents voted for or against the unification of four local municipal governments. The municipalities involved were:

  • The Municipality of the County of Pictou
  • Town of New Glasgow
  • Town of Pictou
  • Town of Stellarton

Summary of Results

Although the results varied by location, the majority of the population voted against amalgamation. Out of a total of 16,683 people who voted:

  • 11029 voted No (66.11%)
  • 5630 voted Yes (33.75%)
  • 24 rejected (0.14%)
